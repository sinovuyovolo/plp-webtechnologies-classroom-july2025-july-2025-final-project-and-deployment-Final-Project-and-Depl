Our Story

Debug Farmstead is more than just a farm; it's a dream brought to life by two young ladies from Soweto, Sinovuyo and Refiloe**. Having grown up in the city, they developed a passion for sustainable living and ethical agriculture. They decided to leave the bustling city life and establish their own farm in the serene landscape of Queenstown, Eastern Cape. Their journey is a testament to their dedication to providing healthy, high-quality produce and livestock to their community.

Meet the Owners

Sinovuyo

Sinovuyo is the visionary behind the farm's innovative farming techniques. With a background in environmental science, she focuses on sustainable practices and maximizing yield while minimizing environmental impact.

Refiloe

Refiloe is the heart of the farm, managing community relations and business operations. Her passion lies in creating a space that connects people with their food and the land it comes from.
